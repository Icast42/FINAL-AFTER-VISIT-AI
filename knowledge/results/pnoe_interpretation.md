# PNOĒ / Results Interpretation – Deep, Emotional Pitch

PNOĒ is **much more than a fitness test**.

It is a metabolic and respiratory assessment that:
- Shows how well your **heart, lungs, and metabolism** are working together
- Reveals how your body **burns fuel** (carbs vs fats) at different intensities
- Estimates how your body is **aging biologically** from a cardiorespiratory standpoint
- Gives clarity on **calories and macros** so you’re not guessing

The goal is to help guests feel like:
- “I finally understand what my body is doing.”
- “I know what to do next instead of guessing.”

## How to Emotionally Explain PNOĒ

When you recommend PNOĒ, your explanation should sound like this (adapt and personalize):

> “PNOĒ is more than just a fitness report — it actually shows us how your body is aging on the inside, not just the number on your birthday.  
> It measures how well your heart, lungs, and metabolism are working together, and how efficiently you use oxygen and burn fuel.  
> 
> From that, we can see your **metabolic health**, an estimate of your **biological age**, and real numbers for how many **calories** you need and how your **macros** (proteins, carbs, fats) should be structured.  
> 
> Instead of guessing at diets and workouts, you get a personalized roadmap for your nutrition, training, recovery, and long-term health — so you’re not just hoping you’ll feel better, you actually know how to get there.”

### Tie PNOĒ Directly to Their WHY

Always connect PNOĒ to the deeper reason they gave you:

- **Family / kids**
  > “This gives us a way to protect your energy and your long-term health, so you can be present with them longer — not just this month, but years from now.”

- **Aging / longevity**
  > “This helps us see where your biological health really is today, so we can make course corrections now instead of waiting for problems later.”

- **Performance / high achiever**
  > “This lets us dial in your training and recovery so your effort actually pays off — you’re not just working harder, you’re working smarter based on your own data.”

- **Weight loss / body change**
  > “Instead of randomly cutting calories or trying diets, we can see how your body uses fuel and build a plan that fits your metabolism, not someone else’s.”

## Using PNOĒ to Guide Nutrition (Non-Medical, Educational)

You are **not** giving medical advice. You are giving **general, educational guidance** based on their goals.

You may:
- Talk about **calorie ranges** (for loss, maintenance, gain)
- Suggest general **macro splits** such as:
  - Fat loss: 20–30% protein, 30–35% fat, 35–50% carbs
  - Muscle gain: higher protein (25–35%), carbs to support training, moderate fats
- Encourage:
  - Whole foods, lean proteins, high-fiber carbs, healthy fats
  - Hydration and reducing ultra-processed foods

You must always include a reminder like:
> “This is general wellness guidance, not a medical prescription. For specific conditions or medical questions, follow your healthcare provider’s advice.”

## Using PNOĒ to Guide Training (Non-Medical)

Based on their PNOĒ data and goals, you can outline a simple **weekly structure**, for example:

- 3–4 days per week: strength training (full body or split)
- 2–3 days per week: cardio in their optimal zones (easy + moderate; possibly one higher-intensity day if appropriate)
- 1 day per week: active recovery (walking, light movement, mobility, sauna, etc.)

Emphasize:
- Training in the **correct zones** (not always going as hard as possible)
- Consistency > perfection
- Recovery and sleep as part of the plan

Again:
> “This is general fitness guidance, not rehab or medical advice.”

## Sample 7-Day High-Level Structure

You may outline a sample week such as:

- **Day 1**: Strength (full body)
- **Day 2**: Zone 2–style cardio
- **Day 3**: Strength (upper / lower split)
- **Day 4**: Light cardio or intervals (if appropriate)
- **Day 5**: Strength
- **Day 6**: Active recovery + sauna / recovery services
- **Day 7**: Rest or gentle movement

Pair that with:
- A consistent calorie/macro target informed by PNOĒ
- Guidance on pre/post-workout fuel
- DripBar services for energy and recovery
